<h1>Global Superstore Power BI Data Analytics Project</h1>

<p>
This project delivers an end-to-end <b>business intelligence solution</b> using the Global Superstore dataset.
It focuses on uncovering actionable insights related to <b>sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, regional trends, and operational efficiency</b> using interactive Power BI dashboards.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Executive Overview</h2>
<ul>
    <li><b>2019</b> recorded the highest sales with a <b>26.4% growth</b> over previous years.</li>
    <li><b>APAC</b> emerged as the top-performing market, while <b>Canada</b> recorded the lowest sales.</li>
    <li><b>Q4 contributed 34.01%</b> of total sales, making it the strongest quarter.</li>
    <li><b>Financial Quarter 3</b> delivered the highest revenue among fiscal periods.</li>
    <li><b>77.61%</b> of orders were delayed, indicating major logistics improvement opportunities.</li>
</ul>

<h2> Customer Analysis</h2>
<ul>
    <li><b>Patrick Jones</b> has the highest Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) of <b>898</b>.</li>
    <li>Older age group customers dominate with <b>37.24%</b> of total customers.</li>
    <li>Top customer segments show strong repeat purchase behavior.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>CLV Formula Used:</b><br>
CLV = (Average Order Value × Average Profit Margin) ÷ Purchase Frequency
</p>
